About Whelmed

I always find that when I have a ton of tasks to do, I have a really hard time distinguishing between which ones are important and which ones would be okay falling by the wayside. This eventually leads to a build up of tasks to a point where I feel overwhelmed by all the work I have to do. Whelmed helps combat this feeling. When adding tasks to a list, the user is asked to think about the positive and negative consequences of not finishing a task on time. This helps them think through which tasks may be more valuable to accomplish than others and gives them a starting place when they feel overwhelmed.
